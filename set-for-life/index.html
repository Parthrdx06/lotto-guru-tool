What Makes Set for Life Unique?

Set for Life is Australia's only daily lottery game that offers an annuity prize. Unlike Powerball or Oz Lotto, which pay out millions in one go, the Set for Life Division 1 prize is $20,000 every single month for 20 years. This creates a long-term financial safety net that winners often describe as "winning every month."

The draw takes place every night at 9:00pm AEST. To play, you select 7 numbers from 1 to 44. During the draw, 9 balls are selected: the first 7 are the winning numbers and the final 2 are supplementary numbers.

The Division 2 Advantage

A major draw for many players is the unique Division 2 prize. If you match 6 main numbers and one supplementary number, you win $5,000 a month for an entire year. This "bonus" annuity makes the secondary prize tier much more exciting than the standard one-off payments found in other games.
