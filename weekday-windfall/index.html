What is Weekday Windfall?

Weekday Windfall is the newest addition to the Australian lottery lineup, replacing the traditional Monday & Wednesday Lotto. This exciting draw now takes place three nights a week: Monday, Wednesday, and Friday. It offers a unique value proposition: a guaranteed $1 Million top prize for up to six winners in every draw.

The game follows the popular 6/45 format. To play, you select six numbers from 1 to 45. During the draw, eight balls are selected at random. The first six are the winning numbers, while the final two are supplementary numbers used to determine lower-tier prizes.

Winning a Million Dollars

Unlike the major jackpot games like Powerball, where the prize pool fluctuates, Weekday Windfall is designed for consistency. By matching all 6 main numbers, you qualify for the $1 Million prize. If there are more than six Division 1 winners, the total prize pool of $6 Million is shared equally among them.

With draws now including Friday, players have a consistent opportunity to become a millionaire at the start, middle, and end of the working week. The odds of hitting the jackpot are 1 in 8,145,060, making it one of the most accessible millionaire-making games in Australia.

Smart Strategies for Play

Many players prefer Weekday Windfall because of its fixed prize structure and reliable draw schedule. Using a random number generator, like the one provided above, ensures that your picks are not influenced by common patterns or lucky numbers that many other players might be using. This reduces the chance of having to share a Division 1 prize with multiple people.
