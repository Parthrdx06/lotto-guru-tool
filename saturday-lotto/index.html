About Saturday Lotto

Saturday Lotto, also known as TattsLotto, Gold Lotto, or X Lotto depending on your state, is Australiaâ€™s most popular weekly draw. It is famous for its "Superdraws" which can see the jackpot rise to $20 million or even $30 million several times a year.

The game format is simple: players choose 6 numbers from a pool of 1 to 45. During the draw, 8 balls are selected. The first 6 balls are the "winning numbers" and the last 2 balls are the "supplementary numbers." To win the Division 1 jackpot, you must match all 6 main winning numbers.

Why Play Saturday Lotto?

Compared to games like Powerball or Oz Lotto, Saturday Lotto offers significantly better odds of winning the Division 1 prize. At approximately 1 in 8.1 million, your chance of becoming a millionaire on a Saturday is nearly 16 times higher than in a typical Powerball draw.

The game also features an excellent overall win rate. The odds of winning any prize are approximately 1 in 85. This makes it a favorite for "syndicate" entries where groups of players pool their money to buy more entries and increase their chances further.
