LottoGuru.net was created by an independent Australian developer to provide a simple, fast, and completely random number generation tool for popular Australian lotteries.

Our Goal

Our goal is not to predict the lottery, but to provide unbiased, quick-pick numbers based on the official rules of each game, helping you avoid biases when selecting your own numbers. We believe the process of choosing numbers should be fun and easy!

Contact Us

If you have any questions, feedback, or need support regarding the tool, please contact us directly via email. We aim to respond within 48 hours.
