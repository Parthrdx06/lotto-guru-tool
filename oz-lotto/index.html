How to Play Oz Lotto

Oz Lotto is Australia's first national lottery, famous for its Tuesday night draws and massive "Big Tuesday" jackpots. Following a major game update in May 2022, the game now requires players to select 7 numbers from a pool of 1 to 47.

Every Tuesday night at approximately 8:30pm AEST, 10 balls are drawn from the barrel. The first 7 balls are the main winning numbers used for the Division 1 jackpot, while the final 3 balls are supplementary numbers used to determine prizes in Divisions 2, 4, and 7.

Oz Lotto Prize Tiers & Odds

There are seven different ways to win a prize in Oz Lotto. While the jackpot odds for Division 1 are approximately 1 in 62.8 million, the overall chance of winning any prize on a single line is much higher at 1 in 51.

The minimum winning requirement is just 3 main winning numbers plus 1 supplementary number (Division 7). All winnings from official Australian lottery draws are paid out as tax-free lump sums, though any interest earned on those winnings may be subject to standard income tax.
