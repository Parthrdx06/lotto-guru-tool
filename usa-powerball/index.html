The World's Biggest Lottery

USA Powerball is the undisputed king of lotteries. In November 2022, it set the world record for the largest jackpot in history at $2.04 billion, won by a single ticket holder in California. Even more recently, in late 2025, it produced another massive $1.8 billion winner, cementing its reputation for creating instant billionaires.

How the Game Works

To play Powerball, you select five numbers from a pool of 1 to 69 (the white balls) and one number from a pool of 1 to 26 (the red Powerball). Drawings are held three times a weekâ€”Monday, Wednesday, and Saturdayâ€”at 10:59 p.m. ET in Tallahassee, Florida.

Power Play and Double Play

For an extra $1, players can add the "Power Play" feature, which multiplies non-jackpot prizes by up to 10 times. There is also "Double Play," an add-on that lets you play your numbers in a second drawing immediately following the main one, with a top prize of $10 million. These features make it possible to turn a small $50,000 win into a $500,000 windfall.
