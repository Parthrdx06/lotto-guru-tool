About Powerball Australia

Powerball Australia is the nation's premier lottery game, known for creating multi-millionaires with its massive jackpot pools. To win the Division 1 prize, players must correctly match seven main numbers from a pool of 1 to 35 and the crucial Powerball from a separate pool of 1 to 20.

Managed by The Lott, this game follows a double-drum format, which significantly increases the difficulty of the jackpot but results in much larger prize pools compared to the standard Saturday Lotto. Draws occur every Thursday night at 8:30 PM AEST.

Understanding the Odds

While the odds of hitting the jackpot are 1 in 134 million, Powerball offers nine different prize divisions, making it possible to win with as few as two main numbers and the Powerball.
