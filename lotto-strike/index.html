What is super 66?

super 66 is an exciting add-on game played alongside the Monday, Wednesday, and Saturday Lotto draws (and Weekday Windfall). Unlike the main games where the order doesn't matter, super 66 is all about the exact order in which the balls are drawn from the barrel.

To win the top prize, known as "Strike 4," you must correctly predict the first four balls drawn in their correct sequence. If no one wins Strike 4, the prize money jackpots to the next draw, often leading to significant prize pools.

Ways to Win Strike

You don't have to match all four to win a prize. super 66 offers four prize levels. You can win "Strike 1" simply by matching the very first ball drawn. "Strike 2" and "Strike 3" require you to match the first two or three balls in their exact positions respectively.
