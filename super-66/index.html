What is super-66?

super-66 is a unique lottery game in Australia that is played as an "add-on" to major draws like Saturday Lotto, Monday & Wednesday Lotto, Oz Lotto, and Powerball. Unlike other games where you choose numbers from a pool, in super-66, you are assigned a random six-digit number.

How the Drawing Works

During the draw, six balls are chosen one by one from six separate machines, each containing balls numbered 0 to 9. This means that, unlike other lotteries, the same number can appear multiple times (for example, 5-5-2-1-5-0).

How to Win

To win the Jackpot (Division 1), your six-digit number must match the drawn numbers in the exact same order. However, you can still win smaller prizes by matching the first or last 2, 3, 4, or 5 digits. The beauty of super-66 is that you can win from either end of your number sequence!
