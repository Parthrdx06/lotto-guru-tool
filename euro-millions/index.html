How to Play

Players must select five main numbers from 1 to 50 and two "Lucky Stars" from a separate pool of 1 to 12. During the draw, five main balls are drawn followed by two stars. You win the jackpot by matching all seven numbers. Draws take place every Tuesday and Friday evening in Paris.
